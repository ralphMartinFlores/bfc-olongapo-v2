Our Menu

Supporting Local Farmers, Delivering Quality Coffee: Our Commitment to Philippine Coffee

At our company, we prioritize local farmers and sustainability. We source coffee beans locally, supporting Filipino livelihoods and eco-friendly farming.

We take pride in our coffee-making process, from selecting top-quality beans to perfect roasting, offering affordability and quality.

We believe everyone deserves a great cup of coffee, delivering it conveniently to your doorstep.

Our commitment to Philippine coffee means supporting local farmers, using their high-grade beans, and contributing to our local economy.

The fastest growing cafe franchise in Metro Manila, now available in Olongapo City.

We're known for our high-quality coffee and welcoming ambiance, which has fueled our rapid growth as a cafe franchise in the metropolitan area. Now, we're excited to extend that exceptional service and experience to Olongapo City's residents and visitors.

Our menu boasts a diverse selection of specialty coffee drinks, meticulously prepared, including classic espresso and signature flavored lattes. We also provide an array of pastries and light snacks, ideal for quick breakfasts or midday indulgences.
